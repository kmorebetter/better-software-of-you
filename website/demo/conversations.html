What gets extracted

Every transcript yields six signal types.

Each extraction is automatic. Nothing requires manual tagging or summary — Claude reads the raw transcript and surfaces the signal.

Commitments by party
Yours vs. theirs, auto-linked to contacts and surfaced as CRM nudges when overdue.
Talk ratio
Percentage of airtime per speaker across the call. Trends tracked over time.
Question frequency
Are you asking or telling? Useful signal for sales conversations and coaching dynamics.
Longest monologue
The single longest uninterrupted stretch. A concrete coaching signal for both parties.
Topic clustering
What themes are recurring across multiple calls with this contact or team.
Relationship health signals
Derived from tone patterns, reciprocity, and engagement over time. Not sentiment guessing — pattern-based.
