What gets extracted

Every transcript yields six signal types.

Each extraction is automatic. Nothing requires manual tagging or summary — Claude reads the raw transcript and surfaces the signal.

Commitments by party
Yours vs. theirs, auto-linked to contacts and surfaced as CRM nudges when overdue.
Talk ratio
Percentage of airtime per speaker across the call. Trends tracked over time.
Question frequency
Are you asking or telling? Useful signal for sales conversations and coaching dynamics.
Longest monologue
The single longest uninterrupted stretch. A concrete coaching signal for both parties.
Topic clustering
What themes are recurring across multiple calls with this contact or team.
Relationship health signals
Derived from tone patterns, reciprocity, and engagement over time. Not sentiment guessing — pattern-based.

Connected intelligence

Conversations connect to everything else.

Conversation Intelligence doesn't operate in isolation. Extracted data feeds back into every other module — turning isolated call data into living context.

→ CRM nudges. Extracted commitments surface automatically when they're overdue or when you're about to talk to that person again.
→ Project context. Topics from calls link to active projects — so your project log knows what's been discussed, not just what's been written down.
→ Relationship health scores. Talk ratios and topic patterns feed your overall contact health — so your strongest relationships are visible, and declining ones get flagged early.
→ Journal continuity. Key decisions from calls get surfaced in the decision log. Emotional context can be linked to journal entries from the same period.
